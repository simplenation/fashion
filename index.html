Our Story

Founded in Lagos in 2015, Bellamy Wears began as a small boutique with a vision to redefine African fashion. Our founder, Adesua Bellamy, wanted to create pieces that honor traditional African textiles while appealing to contemporary tastes.

Today, we're proud to be one of Nigeria's leading fashion brands, known for our quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and commitment to sustainable practices.
